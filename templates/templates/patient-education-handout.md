# Patient Education Handout: Basic Pulmonary Rehabilitation Practice

## Purpose
This handout provides a simple starter guide for basic pulmonary rehabilitation practice and recovery-oriented daily self-monitoring.

## Core daily activities
- breathing exercises
- light walking or low-intensity activity
- coughing or airway clearance practice if instructed
- symptom observation
- rest and hydration

## Suggested self-monitoring items
- shortness of breath
- fatigue
- activity tolerance
- sputum clearance
- oxygen saturation if available

## When to seek medical advice
Please seek professional advice if symptoms worsen significantly, including:
- marked shortness of breath
- chest pain
- dizziness
- very low exercise tolerance
- oxygen desaturation if monitored

## Note
This material is only a starter educational template and should not replace individualized clinical advice.
